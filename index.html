Who We Are

Yum-Me Meal Planner is here to help you cook delicious meals with less stress and more joy. Based on your calories and BMR, we offer recipes and cooking advice for everyone.

Yum-Me Meal Planner was founded in 2023 by a team of passionate food lovers who wanted to make healthy cooking simple for home cooks with more than 1,500 tested recipes and meal plans.

We're supported by a talented group of recipe developers, food writers, photographers, and other creative professionals.
